Fundamental Simulation variables:
- Velocity
- Vertical Velocity
- Horizontal Velocity
- Vertical displacement
- Horizontal displacement
- Gravitational constant (9.802m/s^2)
- Time elapsed/Time taken
- Acceleration
- Mass
- Initial Thrust Force
- Normal Thrust Force
- Motor Burn Time

This is excluding the control system and strictly considers a point mass being propelled up. It does not really take much into consideration but is fundamental
